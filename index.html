The charitable association is established for the following charitable purposes:

  1. Bring together the Algerian elites living in Algeria and abroad.
  2. Promote Algerian talent, culture, and excellence in the scientific, economic, artistic, and social fields.
  3. Strengthen the ties between Algeria and its diaspora through initiatives for cooperation and exchange.
  4. Organize conferences, exhibitions, trade shows, and events of a cultural or professional nature that enhance the image of Algeria worldwide.
  5. Encourage education, innovation, and entrepreneurship among youth and Algerian professionals.

üíé Administrators

  1. The charitable association is managed by a Board of Directors (or Board of Administrators) composed of at least three (3) members and at most twelve (12).
  2. The Administrators serve for renewable terms of three (3) years.
  3. The Administrators are appointed by a majority vote of the existing Board.
  4. The Board includes the following positions:
    • Executive President
    • Honorary President (Founder)
    • Vice-President
    • Secretary General
    • Treasurer

üë• Meetings and Decisions

  1. The Board must meet at least twice a year, either in person or virtually.
  2. Decisions are made by a majority vote; in case of a tie, the Executive President has the casting vote.
  3. Minutes of each meeting must be recorded and kept in a safe place.
